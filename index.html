No. Audits and cleanups are flat-fee, one-time engagements. Monthly management is month-to-month with no minimum commitment. Stop at any time.

What access do you need to my Google Ads account?

Read-only access for audits, standard admin access for cleanup and management. You remain the account owner at all times and can revoke access instantly.

Agencies typically charge $1,500–$5,000/month, require 3–6 month contracts, and have a junior analyst managing your account day-to-day. With Clarify you get senior-level work, direct communication, and pricing based on what your account actually needs.
