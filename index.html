06 — FAQ

Straight answers before you send access.

Do I need to commit to monthly management?

No. Audits and cleanups are one-time projects. Management is month-to-month and only makes sense once the account has a clear optimization path.

What access do you need?

Read-only is enough for an audit. Admin access only if you want changes implemented. You remain the owner and can revoke access anytime.

How is this different from an agency?

Agencies bundle paid search into broad retainers with multiple handoffs. Clarify is narrower: diagnosis, cleanup, tracking, reporting, and ongoing optimization when needed.

Do you guarantee results?

No ethical specialist should. The goal is better account quality, measurement, and decision-making so your spend has a better chance of working.

Hire Clarify or use the software?

The software surfaces issues and alerts you — but you act on them yourself. Hiring Clarify adds the expertise: prioritization, careful implementation, and testing judgment the software can't replace. Software keeps you informed; management gets it handled.
