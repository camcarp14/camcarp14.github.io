Diagnosis

Most accounts do not need more spend. They need less waste.

Small business Google Ads accounts usually break in predictable places: tracking, structure, query quality, landing page alignment, and lack of disciplined optimization. Clarify is built around finding those breaks quickly.

Leak · L-01

Keywords are too loose.

Broad match and automated campaign types can work, but not when they are allowed to chase low-intent searches without negatives, exclusions, or signal quality.

Leak · L-02

Tracking is lying.

If conversions are duplicated, missing, or poorly weighted, the bidding system optimizes toward bad data and makes every future decision harder.

Leak · L-03

No one owns the account logic.

Campaigns get patched over time. Budgets move. Tests disappear. Eventually no one knows why the account is structured the way it is.

Leak · L-04

Reporting does not answer the real question.

Clicks and cost are not strategy. You need a view of spend, demand, conversion quality, and what should change next.

FAQ

Straight answers before you send access.

Do I need to commit to monthly management?

No. Audits and cleanups are one-time projects. Monthly management is month-to-month and only makes sense after the account has a clear optimization path.

What access do you need?

Read-only access is enough for an audit. Admin access is only needed when you want Clarify to implement account changes. You remain the owner and can revoke access at any time.

How is this different from an agency?

Agencies often bundle paid search into a broader retainer with multiple handoffs. Clarify is narrower: Google Ads diagnosis, cleanup, tracking review, reporting, and ongoing paid search optimization when needed.

What kind of businesses are a good fit?

Clarify is best for small businesses already spending on Google Ads, planning to start, or unsure whether their current account is wasting budget. The best fit is a business that values clear documentation and practical next steps.

Do you guarantee better results?

No ethical paid search specialist should guarantee specific performance. The goal is to improve account quality, measurement, structure, and decision-making so your spend has a better chance of working.

What's the difference between hiring Clarify and using the software?

The software runs the same audit logic and lead-outreach system, but you're in the driver's seat — you connect your own account and act on what it finds. Hiring Clarify means a person does the diagnosis, the fixes, and the outreach for you. Most businesses pick based on time, not budget: if you have an hour a week to spend on it, the software is usually the better value.

What kinds of things can you build?

Custom Google Ads and marketing dashboards are the most common ask, but the underlying skillset is general: internal tools, customer-facing web apps, and API integrations between the software you already use. If it can be built with React, Supabase, and Netlify, it's in scope — bring the problem and we'll figure out if it's a fit.

What should I send before an audit?

Your website, monthly ad spend range, main conversion goal, and what feels broken. Read-only Google Ads access is enough for the actual audit.
